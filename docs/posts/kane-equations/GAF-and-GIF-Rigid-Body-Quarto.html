A multibody system comprised of two uniformly dense thin rods of length \(l\) and mass \(ùëö\). Rod \(ùê¥\) is pinned at \(ùëÇ\) and can rotate about \(\hat{n}_{z}\) through \(q_{1}\). Rod \(B\) is pinned to \(A\) and can rotate relative to \(A\) about \(\hat{a}_{x}\) through \(q_{2}\). Linear torisional springs of stiffnes \(k\) with a free length of zero resists each relative rotation. Gravitational forces are in the \(\hat{n}_{x}\) direction.
